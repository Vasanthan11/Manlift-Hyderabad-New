Why choose scissor lifts?

Benefits of hiring scissor lift for your projects

Scissor lifts provide a safe, stable and cost-effective alternative to scaffolding for many indoor and outdoor applications. Here are some of the reasons clients prefer them.

↑

Straight-up vertical access

Scissor lifts move directly up and down, making them perfect for tasks where work is done straight overhead – such as lighting, HVAC, sprinkler lines, cable trays, racks and ceilings.

Typical use: warehouses, atriums, production floors, retail fit-outs.

⚙

Large platform & load capacity

Wide platforms allow two or more people to work together with tools, ducting, cable drums, false ceiling materials and lighting fixtures – reducing the number of trips up and down.

Platform capacity options to suit your material handling needs.

✔

Safe, quick & cost-efficient

Lifts can be driven between work areas, set up in minutes and repositioned easily, helping you cut down on labour and time compared to conventional scaffolding.

Ideal for repetitive maintenance points spread across a facility.

Scissor lift range

Types of scissor lifts available for rental

Select from electric, diesel and rough-terrain scissor lifts depending on height, load capacity, indoor/outdoor use and floor conditions.

  • Electric slab scissor lifts – compact footprint, non-marking tyres and zero emissions for indoor warehouses, showrooms, malls and airports.
  • Diesel rough-terrain scissor lifts – four-wheel drive, outriggers on some models and high ground clearance for outdoor and semi-finished sites.
  • Narrow-aisle scissor lifts – designed to navigate between pallet racks and shelving systems where aisle width is limited.
  • High-capacity scissor lifts – larger platforms with higher SWL (safe working load) to carry multiple technicians, components and tools at once.
  • Low-level access lifts – push-around and micro scissor lifts for safe access up to moderate heights in sensitive environments.

Safety & support

How we support safe scissor lift operations

Safety is at the centre of every scissor lift rental. From pre-dispatch inspection to operator briefing and maintenance, our team focuses on reducing risk on site.

Before dispatch

Pre-rental inspection & documentation

Each scissor lift undergoes a pre-rental check covering controls, hydraulics, brakes, emergency lowering and guard rails. We can share inspection checklists and load test documents where applicable.

On arrival

Site handover & familiarisation

Our team or operator walks through emergency controls, safe working load, platform gates, pothole protection, alarms and battery/engine checks with your supervisors before handover.

During use

Breakdown support & preventive care

We schedule preventive maintenance on long-term jobs and provide quick response for breakdown calls so that downtime is minimised and your teams can plan work confidently.

Training

Operator & user guidance

Where required, we can provide trained operators or guidance for your own trained personnel on daily checks, safe operating zones, harness points and safe loading practices.
