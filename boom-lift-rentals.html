Have questions about boom lift rentals?

A quick discussion helps avoid under-sized or over-sized machines on site. Here are a few points we normally clarify before confirming a boom lift.

  • What maximum working height and outreach do you need?
  • Are there obstacles such as canopies, pipes or parapets?
  • Is the work area indoor, outdoor or a mix of both?
  • What is the floor condition and gradient along the travel path?
  • Do you require an operator or will you use your own crew?
